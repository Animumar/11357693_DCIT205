COMPSSA

About Us

Welcome to COMPSSA UoG, the vibrant and dynamic Computer Science Student Association at The University Of Ghana! We are a community of passionate and forward-thinking students dedicated to fostering collaboration, innovation, and growth within the field of computer science.

Mision

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to connect, learn, and thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of computer science. We aim to:

  • Facilitate networking opportunities among students, faculty, and industry professionals.
  • Promote academic excellence and skill development in computer science.
  • Organize events, workshops, and projects to enhance practical knowledge.
  • Foster a sense of community and camaraderie among computer science enthusiasts.

What we offer

  1. Networking Events

    Connect with like-minded individuals, professors, and professionals through our networking events. Expand your circle, share experiences, and build valuable relationships for your future.

  2. Workshops and Tech Talks

    Stay at the forefront of industry trends and advancements with our hands-on workshops and engaging tech talks. Learn from experts and gain practical insights into cutting-edge technologies.

  3. Coding Competitions

    Sharpen your coding skills and compete with peers in friendly coding competitions. Challenge yourself, solve problems, and showcase your talents in a competitive yet supportive environment.

  4. Hackathons

    Participate in hackathons to unleash your creativity and innovation. Collaborate with fellow students to tackle real-world problems and build solutions that make a difference.

  5. Academic Support

    Access academic resources, study groups, and mentorship programs to excel in your computer science studies. CompSSA UoG is here to support your academic journey.
